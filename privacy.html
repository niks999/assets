Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 9, 2025

1. Introduction

Calendar Conflict Auto-Decliner ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Google Workspace add-on.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Calendar Data

Our add-on accesses your Google Calendar events to:

  • Identify scheduling conflicts between your accepted and pending meetings
  • Read event details (title, description, attendees, time) for conflict detection
  • Automatically decline conflicting calendar invitations based on your configured rules

2.2 Configuration Data

We store your add-on configuration settings, including:

  • Protected keywords list
  • Lookback and lookahead day preferences
  • Minimum meeting duration settings
  • Automatic processing preferences

2.3 User Information

We access your email address to identify your own meetings and properly handle calendar event responses.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Detecting scheduling conflicts in your calendar
  • Automatically declining conflicting meetings according to your preferences
  • Storing and retrieving your configuration settings
  • Providing the core functionality of the add-on

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All configuration data is stored locally within your Google account using Google's PropertiesService. We do not store your data on external servers.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security practices:

  • Use of minimal OAuth scopes (only calendar.events access)
  • No external data transmission or storage
  • Secure Google Apps Script platform hosting
  • Regular security reviews and updates

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Share your calendar data with third parties
  • Sell or rent your personal information
  • Store your data outside of Google's secure infrastructure
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes

6. Data Retention

Configuration settings are retained until you:

  • Uninstall the add-on
  • Manually clear your settings within the add-on
  • Delete your Google account

Calendar event data is accessed in real-time and not stored permanently by our add-on.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and modify your configuration settings at any time
  • Disable or uninstall the add-on at any time
  • Review which permissions the add-on has been granted
  • Revoke access to your calendar data through Google's permission settings

8. OAuth Scopes and Permissions

Our add-on requests the following minimal permissions:

  • calendar.events - Read and modify calendar events for conflict detection and auto-declining
  • userinfo.email - Identify your email address for proper event handling
  • calendar.addons.execute - Function as a Google Calendar add-on
  • script.scriptapp - Manage automated triggers for scheduled conflict checking

9. Third-Party Services

Our add-on runs exclusively on Google's Apps Script platform and integrates only with Google Calendar. We do not use any external third-party services or APIs.

10. Children's Privacy

Our add-on is designed for business and professional use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

11. International Users

Our add-on is hosted on Google's global infrastructure. By using our add-on, you consent to the processing of your data in accordance with Google's data handling practices.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Continued use of the add-on after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
