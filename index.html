Automatically Manage Your Calendar Conflicts

A smart Google Workspace add-on that automatically declines conflicting calendar invitations while protecting your important meetings.

Learn More

Key Features

Smart Conflict Detection

Automatically identifies scheduling conflicts between your accepted meetings and new invitations.

Protected Keywords

Safeguards important meetings by recognizing keywords like "1:1", "performance review", and "urgent".

Configurable Settings

Customize lookback/lookahead days, minimum meeting duration, and protected keywords to fit your workflow.

Dry Run Mode

Preview which meetings would be declined before enabling automatic processing.

Own Meeting Protection

Optionally protect meetings you've created from being automatically declined.

Automated Scheduling

Runs automatically at configurable intervals to keep your calendar conflict-free.

How It Works

1

Install & Configure

Install the add-on from Google Workspace Marketplace and configure your preferences in Gmail.

2

Automatic Detection

The add-on continuously monitors your calendar for conflicts between accepted and pending meetings.

3

Smart Declining

Conflicting meetings are automatically declined while protecting important meetings based on your rules.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. Calendar Conflict Auto-Decliner:

  • Only accesses your calendar events for conflict detection
  • Stores configuration settings locally in your Google account
  • Never shares your calendar data with third parties
  • Uses minimal OAuth scopes for enhanced security
